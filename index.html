Open Data

The Newport Data Project is based on the idea that everyone has something to contribute if they have access to the right tools. Open datasets are an important part of that idea. The Newport Data Project advocates opening datasets to the public, and seeks out existing open datasets from local, state, national, and international sources. You can explore these datasets on our Data Portal.

Data Analysis

Data provides a unique way of looking at issues. We look at challenges and opportunities in our communities using data-driven methods to provide fact-based options to community members and local leaders. We post our code on GitHub.
